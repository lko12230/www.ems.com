Employee Leave Policy

1. Annual Leave:

  • Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
  • Leave requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance.
  • Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days.

2. Sick Leave:

  • Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave annually.
  • Medical certification is required for absences longer than two consecutive days.

3. Maternity & Paternity Leave:

  • Female employees are entitled to 16 weeks of paid maternity leave.
  • Male employees are entitled to 2 weeks of paid paternity leave.

4. Unpaid Leave:

  • Unpaid leave requests are subject to managerial approval.
  • Extended leave beyond entitlement may impact benefits and service duration.
